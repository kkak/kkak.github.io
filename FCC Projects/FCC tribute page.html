Timeline of Douglas Adams' Life:

  • 1952: Born in Cambridge, England
  • 1971: Attends St. John's College after being awarded an Exhibition in English
  • 1974: Graduates with a B.A. in English Literature and moves to London
  • 1977: Pitches sci-fi comedy radio series called The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy to BBC Radio 4
  • 1979: The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy (book) is published.
  • 1980: The Restaurant at the End of the Universe is published.
  • 1982: Life, the Universe and Everything is published.
  • 1984: So Long, and Thanks for All the Fish is published.
  • 1992: Mostly Harmless is published.
  • 2001: Dies at the age of 49.
